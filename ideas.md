# Advanced Quantum Phenomena & Experiments

Ideas for TinyAleph-based quantum simulation apps.

## Foundational Quantum Mechanics

### 1. Quantum Entanglement Lab
Bell state preparation, CHSH inequality violation tests, non-local correlation visualization. Demonstrate EPR paradox and quantum non-locality using correlated prime-encoded qubits.

### 2. Decoherence Simulator
Environmental interaction models showing how superpositions decay into classical mixtures. Visualize the transition from quantum to classical behavior through environmental coupling.

### 3. Quantum Zeno/Anti-Zeno Explorer
Interactive demonstration of measurement-frequency effects on state evolution. Show how frequent measurements can freeze (Zeno) or accelerate (Anti-Zeno) quantum transitions.

### 4. Tunneling Dynamics Visualizer
Time-evolution through potential barriers with WKB approximation. Animate wavepacket propagation and transmission/reflection coefficients.

## Quantum Information & Cryptography

### 5. BB84 Protocol Simulator
Complete quantum key distribution with eavesdropper detection. Demonstrate information-theoretic security and the disturbance caused by measurement.

### 6. Quantum Teleportation Demo
State transfer through entanglement with classical communication. Show the full protocol: Bell measurement, classical bits, and state reconstruction.

### 7. Quantum Error Correction Lab
Shor/Steane codes, syndrome measurement, error recovery. Demonstrate how redundant encoding protects quantum information from noise.

## Quantum Algorithms

### 8. Grover's Search Visualizer
Amplitude amplification with oracle marking, quadratic speedup demonstration. Animate the rotation in 2D subspace toward marked states.

### 9. Quantum Fourier Transform Explorer
Phase kickback, periodicity detection. Foundation for Shor's algorithm and quantum phase estimation.

### 10. VQE Molecular Simulator

## Advanced Topics

### 11. Quantum Chaos Explorer
Level statistics, random matrix theory, stadium billiards. Explore the quantum signatures of classical chaos.

### 12. Topological Quantum States
Anyonic braiding, Majorana fermion simulation. Demonstrate non-Abelian statistics and topological protection.

### 13. Adiabatic Quantum Computer
Ground state evolution for optimization problems. Show how slow evolution maintains the ground state.

### 14. Quantum Neural Network Trainer
Parameterized circuits with gradient descent. Variational quantum machine learning.

## Unique TinyAleph Integrations

### 15. Prime-Entangled Semantic Network
Meaning entanglement where measuring one concept collapses related concepts. Explore semantic non-locality.

### 16. Consciousness Collapse Experiment
Observer-dependent state reduction with archetypal attractors. Explore the role of observation in meaning-making.
